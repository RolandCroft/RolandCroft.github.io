To better grow my online presence I'm starting my own personal website, and what better way to kick things off than the old programming trope: Hello World! 

This site will function to serve two purposes. Firstly, to help advertise myself and my work, and secondly, to help document my thoughts and activities via the blog. I welcome all visitors, and hopefully my pursuits can be of some interest to you. Whilst my own interests predominantly lie in Computer Science, the subject matter of this site will not be limited to this. I plan to cover all aspects of my life, attitude and observations. 

The site is powered by GitHub pages and Jekyll, and I was quite surprised by how easy it was. It took me barely an evening to set everything up. However, the site is quite bare bones at the moment, so expact improvements for the future. I must give credit to Hank Quinlan for his [tutorial](http://jmcglone.com/guides/github-pages/).
